Known for her quick-witted comedy, campy performances, and larger-than-life aesthetic, Brita Filter is anything but pure. She dominates NYC nightlife and quenches everyone’s thirst with her six weekly shows.
Other career highlights include performing with Katy Perry on SNL, playing the title role in the Off Broadway show “Bridget Bishop Presents: The Salem Bitch Trials,” winning two NY Nightlife Awards for outstanding achievements, and most recently performing  on the Trafalgar Square main stage for London Pride.
