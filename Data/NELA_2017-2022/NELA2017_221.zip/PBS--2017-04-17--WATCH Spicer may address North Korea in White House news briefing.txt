President Donald Trump’s press secretary Sean Spicer holds the daily news briefing from the White House at 2 p.m. ET today. PBS NewsHour will live stream the briefing.

On the morning of President Donald Trump’s first annual Easter Egg Roll at the White House, press secretary Sean Spicer told “Fox & Friends” that the Chinese government could be move involved in efforts to address North Korea aggression.

“I think that there’s a lot more that the Chinese government — both politically and economically — could be doing,” Spicer said during an interview with “Fox & Friends.”

Spicer’s comments came during Vice President Mike Pence’s scheduled 10-day trip to Asia. While visiting the Demilitarized Zone — the dividing line between North and South Korea — Pence told reporters that he remained hopeful that the Chinese government would use its “extraordinary levers” to pressure leaders in North Korea to stop building nuclear weapons and ballistic missiles.

China, instead, has signaled that its want to resume negotiations with North Korea to lower tensions in the region.
