(INTELLIHUB) Director Oliver Stone’s new 4-part Showtime documentary “The Putin Interviews” aired last week making waves across the political spectrum worldwide.

Highly-produced scenes from the film show Stone interacting with Russian President Vladimir Putin during his four trips to the Russian Federation spanning a two-year period. The final 20-hours of footage was condensed into a cut-up series which aired on cable TV last week.

The famed director recently told the Financial Times that he gave “very little opinion” during the film because he wanted Putin “present his point of view” which is often candid.
