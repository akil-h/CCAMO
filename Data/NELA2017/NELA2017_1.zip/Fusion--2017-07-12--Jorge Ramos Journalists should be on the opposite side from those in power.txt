Jorge Ramos is no stranger to controversy. Whether it’s calling out political leaders for not following through on their policies or taking a stand on issues that matter, he’s not afraid to ask the hard questions. In his recent TED talk, Why journalists have an obligation to challenge power, Ramos sheds light on the true role and responsibility of journalists and the need to sometimes take sides.
